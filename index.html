Yoga for Beginners

So you want to get start a yoga practice? There may be many reason why you have shown interest in yoga. Maybe you play other sports or activities that you think might be beneficial to add flexibility or strength.

You also might have an existing injury or just want to get a good workout! Also you might be interested in learning more about meditation and mindfulness. Well, lucky you, you came to the right place!

Benefits of Yoga

  • Improves flexibility
  • Builds muscle strength and awareness
  • Increases blood flow
  • Drops blood pressure
  • Improves balance

Days to Practice

We suggest you make a schedule that allows you to practice at least 3 times a day. You should see benefits listed above show after one month
